History of Food

The history of food is a fascinating journey through human culture and evolution. From ancient agricultural practices to the development of diverse cuisines across the world, food has always been at the center of human civilization. Early humans foraged for wild plants and hunted animals, which eventually led to the domestication of crops and livestock. As societies evolved, so did their culinary practices, influenced by geography, climate, and trade. Spices, cooking techniques, and food preservation methods have all contributed to the rich tapestry of global cuisine we enjoy today.
